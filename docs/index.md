## About me

With over 25 years in healthcare finance, I've developed into an all-round Business Controller and transitioned into Business Intelligence in 2023. These are my personal notes — feel free to use them, and feedback is always welcome.

## Inspired by

Inspired by [Nicole van der Hoeven](https://nicolevanderhoeven.com) and her philosophy of *"Doing It in Public"* — sharing notes in all their messiness, as a work in progress. I started sharing notes from januari 2024. Not waiting until it's perfect, but learning by doing and sharing along the way.
