# Learning in Public

Inspired by [Nicole van der Hoeven](https://nicolevanderhoeven.com) 
and her philosophy of *"Doing It in Public"* — sharing notes in all 
their messiness, as a work in progress. Not waiting until it's perfect, 
but learning by doing and sharing along the way.

## About Me

With over 25 years in healthcare finance, I've developed into an all-round business controller and transitioned into Business Intelligence in 2023. Leveraging my domain knowledge and experience with healthcare providers, I translate data into meaningful insights. I'm still refining my technical skills, but my passion and practical mindset have helped me grow quickly.

Based in Groningen, with a supportive partner and a junior who keeps me on my toes, I take a no-nonsense approach to data solutions, focusing on projects that truly matter. Outside of work, I enjoy community volunteering and training my dogs—two things that keep me connected and grounded.
